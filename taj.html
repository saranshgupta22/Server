The Taj Mahal: A Monument of Love

The Taj Mahal, an epitome of love and architectural beauty, is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, this UNESCO World Heritage Site attracts millions of visitors every year. The grandeur of the white marble structure, intricate carvings, and serene gardens leave an everlasting impression on its visitors.

How to Reach Taj Mahal

  1. By Air: The nearest airport is Agra Airport, located 13 km from the city center.
  2. By Train: Agra is well-connected by rail, with Agra Cantt being the main railway station.
  3. By Road: Agra has excellent road connectivity, accessible via the Yamuna Expressway from Delhi.

Where to Stay Near Taj Mahal

  1. Luxury Hotels: The Oberoi Amarvilas offers unparalleled luxury with views of the Taj Mahal.
  2. Mid-Range Hotels: Trident Agra provides comfortable stays with modern amenities.
  3. Budget Accommodations: There are several guesthouses and budget hotels near the Taj Mahal.
